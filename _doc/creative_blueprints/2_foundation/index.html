The Opportunity

Why Trip Planning Breaks Down

The Problem

Fragmented, slow, and impersonal

Travelers cross-reference dozens of tabs — maps, review sites, booking platforms, travel blogs — and still end up with a generic itinerary that ignores their budget, interests, and pace.

  • Hours lost researching across fragmented sources
  • Generic plans that ignore personal interests and budget
  • No visual overview — no timeline, no map
The Solution

One conversation. A complete, personalized travel plan.

Users describe destination, budget, and interests. The AI generates a full itinerary — hotels, attractions, daily activities — and displays it as a visual timeline and interactive map. Adjust anything with a single message.

  • Personalized to destination, budget, and interests
  • Hotels, attractions, daily schedule — all in one place
  • Adaptive — AI re-plans on request, instantly
