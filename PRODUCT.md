# AI Travel Planner

## Product Overview
A personal AI travel agent that creates complete, personalized travel itineraries based on user-stated destination, budget, and interests.

## Target Users
- Solo travelers and small groups (families, friends)
- Anyone who finds trip planning time-consuming, fragmented, and overwhelming

## Core Problem
Planning a trip manually is slow, fragmented, and impersonal. Travelers cross-reference dozens of sources and still end up with generic itineraries that ignore their personal budget, pace, and interests.

## Core Features
1. **Conversational Input**: Users describe destination, budget range, travel dates, and interests in natural language — no forms, no dropdowns
2. **AI Itinerary Generation**: Complete day-by-day travel plan produced instantly: hotels, attractions, daily activities — personalized to user preferences and budget
3. **Hotel Recommendations**: Accommodation suggestions matched to budget tier and travel style
4. **Attraction & Activity Discovery**: Curated local recommendations aligned with stated interests (culture, adventure, culinary, nature, etc.)
5. **Timeline View**: Visual day-by-day timeline of the full itinerary — every activity and stop laid out in sequence
6. **Interactive Map**: Geographic visualization of all planned stops and routes
7. **Adaptive Planning**: AI listens to feedback and re-generates or adjusts any part of the plan on request

## Tone & Brand
- Warm, knowledgeable, helpful — like a trusted travel-savvy friend
- Visual and exploratory — destinations feel vivid and exciting
- Clean, accessible, optimized for ease of use

## MVP Scope
- Input: destination, budget range, travel dates, personal interests
- Output: full itinerary (hotels, attractions, daily activities) rendered as timeline + interactive map
- Feedback loop: user requests adjustments; AI regenerates
- Out of scope: flight booking, direct hotel/activity booking, payment processing

## Language
- Primary UI language: Indonesian (Bahasa Indonesia)
- App should support bilingual content where appropriate
